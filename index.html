Menu

Antipasti:
1. Bruschetta al Pomodoro: Toasted bread topped with diced tomatoes, garlic, basil, and drizzled with extra virgin olive oil.
2. Arancini Siciliani: Crispy risotto balls stuffed with mozzarella cheese and served with marinara sauce.
3. Caprese Salad: Fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil seasoned with salt, pepper, and balsamic glaze.
Dolci:
1. Tiramisù: Layers of espresso-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cream, dusted with cocoa powder.
2. Cannoli Siciliani: Crispy pastry shells filled with sweet ricotta cheese and chocolate chips, garnished with powdered sugar.
3. Panna Cotta al Frutti di Bosco: Creamy vanilla bean pudding served with mixed berry compote.
